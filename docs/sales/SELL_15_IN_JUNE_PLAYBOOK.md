# Sell 15 Content Agent Systems In June

Goal: sell 7 in the next 15 days, then 15 total in June.

## Offer

Sell this as a done-for-you AI Content Command Center.

Promise:
- Turn raw expertise, calls, videos, and notes into client-ready content packs.
- Includes source checks, brand memory, multi-format outputs, and performance learning.
- Built for founder-led companies that need authority, leads, and speed.

Starter price:
- Setup: $2,500 to $5,000.
- Monthly: $750 to $2,000 for ongoing content system management.

Fast close offer:
- "I will build your first client-specific command center and deliver 10 proof-backed content packs in 14 days."

## Best Leads

Prioritize people who already need content and have money:
- B2B agencies.
- AI consultants.
- SaaS founders.
- Coaches with high-ticket offers.
- Local service companies doing $1M+.
- Recruiters, brokers, and consultants.
- Operators posting inconsistently but selling high-ticket services.

Lead signals:
- Founder posts 1-3x per month but should post more.
- Has case studies, calls, or proof but no content system.
- Has a clear offer above $2,000.
- Team is small and founder is the bottleneck.
- Recent launch, event, webinar, podcast, or newsletter.

## Daily Math

For 7 sales in 15 days:
- 60 targeted DMs per day.
- 15 replies per day target.
- 5 booked calls per day target.
- 1 close every 2 days.

Do not spray generic DMs. Send specific proof.

## DM Opener

Use this after checking their profile:

"Saw your post about [specific topic]. You have enough raw expertise to be posting 5x more without sounding generic. I am building AI content command centers that turn calls, notes, and videos into source-checked LinkedIn posts, threads, emails, and lead magnets. Want me to show you what that would look like for your business?"

## Second DM

"The useful part is not just AI writing. It is the system: research, organize, optimize, write, source check, edit, tone check, engagement check. Then it learns from edits and performance. I can build a lightweight version around your offer."

## Voice Note Script

"Quick idea. You already have the hard part: expertise and proof. The gap is turning that into repeatable content without you staring at a blank page. I can build you a client-specific AI content command center. It takes your calls, notes, videos, or raw ideas and outputs a full content pack with source checks, CTA, and follow-up assets. If useful, I can mock up one example for your business."

## Call Structure

1. Ask what they sell and average deal size.
2. Ask where content comes from now.
3. Ask how much founder time content takes weekly.
4. Ask what happens after someone engages.
5. Show the workflow: source input, brief approval, agent artifacts, pack modal, client handoff.
6. Pitch a 14-day build.
7. Close with setup fee plus monthly support.

## Close Script

"This is not a content calendar. It is an operating system for turning your expertise into proof-backed assets. I can build the first version in 14 days. You get the workspace, brand memory, agent pipeline, and 10 content packs. Setup is $3,500. If you want me to manage the system after, it is $1,250/month."

## Fulfillment Checklist

Day 1:
- Create client workspace.
- Add offer, audience, proof, voice, banned phrases.
- Collect 3 calls, 3 posts, 3 proof assets.

Days 2-4:
- Build first 5 content packs.
- Edit with client voice.
- Save learnings into Brand Memory.

Days 5-7:
- Build Market Radar list.
- Score 20 ideas.
- Pick 10 assets to ship.

Days 8-14:
- Ship content.
- Track replies, DMs, booked calls, revenue.
- Save performance into the pack.
- Generate next sprint from winning patterns.
